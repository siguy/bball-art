# Court & Covenant - Brand Style Guide

## Brand Identity

**Name**: Court & Covenant
**Tagline**: Where legends meet legacy

## Logo

### Primary Logo
- **Style**: Sophisticated gold script
- **Feel**: Luxury brand meets sports heritage
- **Usage**: Card overlays, website header, social profiles

### Logo Variations
1. **Light** (gold on dark) - For cards with dark backgrounds
2. **Dark** (dark gold/bronze on light) - For cards with light backgrounds
3. **Monochrome** - For single-color applications

### Logo Placement on Cards
- **Primary**: Top center, overlapping figures slightly
- **Alternative**: Bottom corner, integrated with name bar
- **Size**: Should be readable but not dominate

## Typography

### Card Titles (Insert Names)
- **Font style**: Bold, era-appropriate
- **1990s**: Clean sans-serif with drop shadow
- **2020s**: Geometric, minimal

### Player/Figure Names
- **Font style**: Clear, legible
- **Size**: Large enough to read at card size
- **Placement**: Bottom bar

### "Court & Covenant" Logo Text
- **Font style**: Elegant script
- **Color**: Covenant Gold

## Card Dimensions

### Standard Card
- **Ratio**: 2.5 x 3.5 inches (standard trading card)
- **Digital**: 750 x 1050 pixels minimum
- **High-res**: 1500 x 2100 pixels for print

## Tone of Voice

### We Are
- Irreverent but respectful
- Self-aware about the absurdity
- Basketball nerds who know their history
- Witty, not try-hard

### We Are Not
- Preachy or reverent
- Corporate or formal
- Explaining the joke
- Taking ourselves too seriously

### Example Captions
Good:
- "Moses parted the Red Sea. MJ parted defenders. Same energy."
- "Scottie and Aaron: Elite support, criminally underrated."

Bad:
- "In this card, we see the parallel between..."
- "Like and follow for more content!"

## Visual Style by Era

### 1990s Cards
- Electric gradients (purple/blue/teal)
- Cosmic backgrounds (nebulae, stars)
- Lightning bolts, energy effects
- Bold sans-serif typography
- High gloss "refractor" finish effect

### 2000s Cards
- Chrome and metallic finishes
- Die-cut effects
- Acetate/translucent looks
- Luxury patch window style
- Y2K futurism

### 2010s-2020s Cards
- Clean geometric patterns
- Silver/holographic effects
- Minimalist design
- Prizm-style shimmer
- Modern typography
