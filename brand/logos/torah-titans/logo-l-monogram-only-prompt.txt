A premium logo element on a TRANSPARENT background in 1:1 aspect ratio.

=== DESIGN CONCEPT ===
JUST THE MONOGRAM - isolated "TT" element for "TORAH TITANS" branding.
Two T letters interlocking elegantly.
Premium gold aesthetic. NO background - transparent/empty.

=== THE MONOGRAM ===
Stylized double-T:
- Two capital letter T's
- Interlocking or overlapping artistically
- Premium gold with embossed appearance
- Art deco or classic serif styling
- Balanced, symmetrical design
- Could mirror each other or stack creatively

=== IMPORTANT ===
- NO background color - must be transparent/empty
- NO circle, seal, or frame around it
- NO other text
- JUST the two golden T letters
- Clean, isolated graphic element

=== COLOR ===
- Rich gold (primary)
- Darker gold shadows for depth
- Bright highlights
- TRANSPARENT background (no black, no color)

=== STYLE ===
- Premium luxury monogram
- Think high-end fashion branding
- Elegant letterforms
- Scalable icon
