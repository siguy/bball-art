A premium logo design on a pure black background in 1:1 aspect ratio.

Interlocking "TT" monogram as a luxury wax seal / medallion.
Inspired by luxury fashion branding and tarot deck back designs.
Premium gold aesthetic throughout.

Stylized double-T forming an elegant interlock:
- "TT" letters merge artistically
- Premium gold with embossed/raised appearance
- Art deco or classic serif styling
- Could suggest Hebrew ת shapes subtly
- Balanced, symmetrical design

Circular medallion framing the monogram:
- Wax seal aesthetic (raised, dimensional)
- Outer ring with subtle decorative border

- Feels like something you'd stamp on premium goods
